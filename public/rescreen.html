Welcome!

This will be your screen to arrange you videos on. Please drag this tab to your projector or second screen/monitor if you're using one, then enter full screen mode.

You can see your shapes drawn on this screen. They will fade away after a few seconds and can be seen again by moving your mouse.

To arrange your shapes, drag the shape's corners to where you want them to be. Use the space bar to toggle between the red input layer, and blue output layer. You currently selected shape/corner will be yellow

If there is more than one corner or shape overlapping, you can cycle through them by holding shift while you click

Additionally, you can move the selected corner using the arrow keys on your keyboard, and you can cycle through the selected shape's corners by hold shift an using the arrow keys. You can also cycle through the shapes on the screen by holding shift and the up or down keys

To see this message again after you close it, just press H on your keyboard

Have fun!
